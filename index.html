Ultimate Salon ("we", "our", "us") is a dual-mode app that helps users discover and book salon services and restaurant reservations based on user-selected preferences (such as style, budget, and city). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the Ultimate Salon mobile application (“App”).

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Users can browse parts of the App without creating an account. We collect information only when you provide it or when needed for booking/reservations and app functionality.

A. Account Information (Optional)

  • At Sign-Up: name, email address, password, and city.
  • Profile Photo: optional (you may add a photo, or skip).

B. Preference & Personalization Information

To tailor the experience, the App may collect preferences you select, such as:

  • Salon preferences: style category, services, purpose, mood/vibe, budget range, and other details you choose.
  • Restaurant preferences: cuisines, budget range, dining preferences, and other details you choose.
  • City selection: used to show location-based recommendations.

C. Booking & Reservation Information

If you book a salon appointment or reserve a restaurant table, we may collect details needed to complete the request, such as:

  • Selected salon/artist or restaurant, date/time, number of guests (restaurant), and related notes.
  • Contact details you provide during booking (for example, name and email; additional fields may be requested depending on booking flow).

D. Payment Proof (If Used)

If your app flow allows users to submit payment proof (for example, a QR payment confirmation screenshot), we may collect:

  • Uploaded image/file containing payment proof and related booking reference details.

Note: Ultimate Salon does not process card payments inside the app at this time.

E. Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, and basic diagnostics (for stability and bug fixes).
  • Log data related to app performance (for example, crash reports if a crash occurs).

F. Location Data (Optional)

  • City-based experience: the App can work using only the city you select.
  • Precise location (if you allow it): used only while the app is open to improve nearby recommendations.
  • No background tracking: we do not collect location in the background.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide the service: show salon and restaurant recommendations based on your selected city and preferences.
  • Enable booking/reservations: process salon bookings and restaurant table reservations.
  • Personalization: tailor content such as recommended salons, stylists, services, cuisines, and restaurants.
  • Customer support: respond to your questions or issues.
  • Notifications: send booking/reservation updates and important service messages (if you opt in).
  • App improvement & security: diagnose crashes, prevent abuse, and maintain reliable performance.

3. Recommendations & Chat (No External AI)

Recommendations: Ultimate Salon uses rule-based logic and your selected preferences (for example, city, style, budget, cuisine) to suggest salons and restaurants. We do not send your personalization data to external AI providers for processing.

In-app chatbot: The App may include a chatbot experience to guide users. Chat is designed to function within the App and is not intended to transmit your chat content to third-party AI services.

Note: If you later integrate third-party AI or analytics, this policy should be updated before release.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only as needed to operate the App, for example:

  • Service providers: cloud hosting, databases, authentication, and push notifications (these providers process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards).
  • Booking partners (if applicable): if a salon/restaurant needs booking details to confirm your reservation.
  • Legal & safety: if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect users, our rights, or public safety.

5. Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit (e.g., HTTPS/TLS where applicable).
  • We apply access controls to protect user information.
  • No system is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and reduce risks.

6. Permissions & Consent

The App may request permissions. You can grant or revoke them in your device settings.

  • Location (Optional): used only while the App is open to improve nearby recommendations. The App also works using city selection only.
  • Photos/Files (Optional): used only if you upload a profile picture or submit payment proof.
  • Notifications (Optional): used to send booking/reservation updates and reminders.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the App and comply with legal obligations.

Delete Account (In-App): You can delete your account in the App. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated personalization data from our systems, subject to limited retention required for legal, security, or dispute purposes.

8. Age Requirements

The App is intended for users who are 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has provided personal data, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. Your Rights

  • Access & update: you may update your profile information in the App.
  • Deletion: you may delete your account using in-app deletion or the web deletion link.
  • Support request: you can contact us to request help with your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will provide notice within the App or by other appropriate means. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
