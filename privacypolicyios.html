Privacy Policy – Ultimate Salon

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Welcome to Ultimate Salon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (“App”). We comply with Apple App Store guidelines and applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information (Optional): Name and email address when you create an account.
  • City Selection: Used to show location-based recommendations.
  • Profile Photo (Optional): If you choose to upload one.
  • Booking Information: Appointment or reservation details when you use booking features.
  • Payment Proof (Optional): If you upload a payment confirmation image.
  • Device Information: Basic diagnostic data such as device type and app performance logs.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No background location tracking.
  • No browsing history.
  • No financial or card information.
  • No health data.
  • No contact list access.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide salon and restaurant booking functionality.
  • To personalize recommendations based on your selected city and preferences.
  • To manage user accounts (if created).
  • To send service-related notifications.
  • To improve app stability and performance.

4. Location Usage

Location access is optional. If granted, it is used only while the app is open to improve nearby recommendations. Ultimate Salon does not collect background location data.

5. Chat Feature

The app includes a built-in bot chat for assistance. Chat data remains within the app system and is not shared with third parties.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may be stored securely on cloud infrastructure used to operate the app. No data is shared for advertising purposes.

7. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.
  • Secure authentication methods are used for account login.
  • Access to user data is restricted and protected.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete your account at any time directly within the app using the account deletion feature.

You can also request account deletion through our web page:

Delete Account: https://dikshitbhatta.github.io/Ultimate-Salon---Privacy-policy/deleteaccount.html

Upon deletion request, your personal data will be permanently removed within 7 business days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Age Restriction

Ultimate Salon is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date.
